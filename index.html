BeeStemified Curriculum

Welcome to the BeeStemified Curriculum

The BeeStemified Curriculum is designed to provide K-12 students with engaging and hands-on learning experiences that inspire curiosity, creativity and a love for STEM fields. Within the BeeStemified Curriculum, a wealth of critical and computational problem solving treasures and instructional resources dedicated learner and STEM educators.

The intention is to make STEM education more accessible and inclusive for all learners, regardless of their background and location. With BeeStemified, I systematically eliminate the uncertainties that often accompany the teaching of STEM subjects providing necessary instructional strategies and resources for educators sin STEM thereby empowering learners to see themselves as capable and confident STEM learners.
